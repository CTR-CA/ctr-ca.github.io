About this item

  • The M18 1/2 inch Hammer Drill Driver with Milwaukee 4 pole frameless motor delivers 450 inch lbs. of peak torque; 0–550/0–1700 RPM; and 29000 BPM
  • Includes the M18 1/4-inch Hex Compact Impact Driver featuring a Milwaukee-designed impact mechanism for 1400 inch lbs. of torque
  • DPM Digital Power Management overload protection prevents damage to the tool and battery in abusive situations
  • Compatible with M18 XC High Capacity REDLITHIUM0 batteries and M18 Compact REDLITHIUM batteries
  • Includes M18 2602-20 1/2-inch Hammer Drill Driver; M18 2650–20 1/4-inch Hex Compact Impact Driver; two M18 Compact REDLITHIUM batteries; 1-hour charger; contractor bag
